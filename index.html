Welcome to Cloud Social Work

At Cloud Social Work, we are dedicated to empowering individuals and fostering resilience. About Cloud Social Work

Cloud Social Work offers a wide range of services designed to enhance the quality of life for our clients. We provide Therapeutic Supports for the NDIS for NDIA Managed, Plan Managed and Self Managed participants. As well we provide Social Work services. Our goal is not just to provide immediate assistance, but to build a foundation for sustainable, long-term positive change. Let us partner with you to achieve your goals and reach your full potential.

Our Approach

Our methodology is rooted in the NDIS framework, ensuring that all our services are compliant, effective, and tailored to the individual. We employ evidence-based practices and continuous professional development to stay at the forefront of social work. By fostering strong community connections and advocating for social justice, we strive to create inclusive environments where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

What we do

Training and Professional Development

We are committed to continuous learning and professional growth, ensuring our team is equipped with the latest knowledge and skills to provide the highest standard of care.

Role of Social Workers

Our social workers play a crucial role in assessing needs, developing care plans, and coordinating services to ensure comprehensive support for our clients.

Comprehensive Therapeutic Approaches

We utilise a range of therapeutic modalities to address mental health challenges, emotional regulation, and behavioural issues, fostering holistic well-being.

Advocacy for Social Justice

We advocate fiercely for the rights and needs of our clients, working to dismantle systemic barriers and promote equity and inclusion within the community.

Training and Professional Development

We invest heavily in the ongoing training of our staff, ensuring they are proficient in the latest evidence-based practices and therapeutic techniques.
